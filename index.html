about TRVL

It is time to reinvent travelling. Let us introduce you TRVL platform.

  • Places/Rapid commercialisation leaves you disconnected from the city, culture and not let you explore in a local way
  • Currency exchange is a major concern for travellers going places. Difficulty in using cards and finding ATMs with money is always a challenge
  • SIM cards with high connectivity costs. Few companies sit in the middle of all transactions. 90%+ transactions happens through these companies
  • Oligopolistic nature of intermediaries allow them to extract high transaction fees

One Solution

  • One stop decentralised solution for all your travel needs
  • One common currency for travellers. No hassle for conversion
  • Buy discounted or in bulk ,items services from different vendors (sub coin)
  • High speed consistent internet connection through mesh technology
  • NO centralised intermediaries to extract high transaction fees
